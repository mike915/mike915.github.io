Idea 1: Go through your contact list, call someone you haven't been in touch for a while

A lot of people feel it's awkward and a little nerve racking to try to get back in touch with a friend they haven't spoken to in a while. Don't think like that, just take the phone, and call him/her. That's how the solid relationship built.

Idea 2: Spend some time to do exercise

I'm a man who loves sports very much. My favorite exercise is jogging. Most of the time, I would do jogging alone, and that is a right time for me to clean my mind and remind me to keep going.

Idea 3: Send your long-distance family surprise postcards

I'm always very happy when I receive a postcard from a friend, family or even a stranger. So why not to do the same thing to make others happy?
