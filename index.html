Purpose behind the site

Life is in fact getting easier and easier with uses of technology. However, people are less connected than before, and they may have hard time to find motivation to chase for their dreams, since they might perceive the world is saturated as they live in the Internet based world most of the time. The purpose of this website is to provide motivation, hints to this group of people based on my own experience, and the end goal I want to get is that people would live in a sustainable life that fills with Belief, Hope, and Love!

Call for sustainable practice

Resources are limited! Although there are lots of sustainability proposals and practices, we still tend to over use resources when they are free or relatively cheap. I think this mindset is not right, and we should address it.

What to do when you are bored

There are moments that we are inevitably to feel bored, this is absolutely normal, and trust me if you decide to spend more time on browsing around the Internet, things get worse. So why don't we treat it as an opportunity?

    • Use it to consolidate the relationship
    • Use it to get involve within the community
    • Use it to work on something you like
    • Use it to make you healthy
